## Summary

Fixes regression from #4964: gnoweb Help/Action "Link" button was copying path-relative URL instead of absolute. Two-layer fix: (1) `requestOrigin()` reads `r.Host`/`r.TLS`/`X-Forwarded-{Proto,Host}`, stores in `GnoURL.Origin`, prepended in `buildHelpURL`; (2) client-side fallback in `controller-copy.ts` prepends `window.location.origin` when value starts with `/`.
